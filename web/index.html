      Optimal use:
      Day-to-day, keep Shared SOL unchecked (heaviest Helius
      load, up to ~156 RPCs) unless you need funder / funded wallets. Use
      Fresh and/or Multi-send as needed.
      When you need all three for a mint the first time, check them
      together on one Analyze — fewer total Helius pings than
      three separate Analyzes (holders base ~6 paid once). After that, uncheck
      what you do not need; last known values still show until
      you check the box again for a live re-scan.
      If you hit 429 on free Helius (~10 RPS), wait 30–60s
      (rate limit ≠ monthly credit burn).
